Message from Our Pastor

Beloved, it is with joy that I welcome you to Deeper Life Bible Church Rochester, NY. We are a family where hearts are mended, faith is strengthened, and lives are transformed by the power of God’s Word.

In every service, we strive to create an atmosphere where you can encounter Christ personally, grow in grace, and fulfill your divine purpose. Our mission is to proclaim the unchanging truth of the gospel in a changing world and to see every believer equipped for victorious Christian living.

I invite you to join us in fellowship, in prayer, and in service. Together, we can reflect the love of Christ to our community and beyond, standing as lights in a dark world and instruments of God’s peace.

As you worship with us, may the Lord bless you, guide you, and grant you His abiding presence in every area of your life.

— Pastor Bolaji Thomas
